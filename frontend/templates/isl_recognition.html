ISL Recognition Features

Advanced features designed to make Indian Sign Language recognition accurate, accessible, and educational for everyone.

Real-time Detection

Instant hand gesture recognition with live camera feed processing at 30+ FPS for smooth interaction.

Multi-Camera Support

Switch between multiple cameras seamlessly with automatic device detection and selection.

Interactive Learning

Built-in ISL alphabet learning with practice modes, tips, and real-time feedback for improvement.

Multi-Language Output

How ISL Recognition Works

Simple steps to start using our advanced ISL recognition system.

1

Select Camera

Choose your preferred camera from available devices and grant camera permissions.

2

Start Recognition

Click start and position your hands in front of the camera for optimal detection.

3

Sign Letters

Perform ISL alphabet signs clearly and hold steady for accurate recognition.

4

Get Translation

View real-time text output and multi-language translations of your signs.
