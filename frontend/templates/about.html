ISL Recognition

Wave your hands, get words instantly. Our AI watches your signs and translates them in real-time. Works with both hands, understands Indian Sign Language, and gets better the more you use it.

Voice Tools

Talk to your computer, watch it type. Type something, hear it spoken back. Great for accessibility, note-taking, or just showing off to friends.
